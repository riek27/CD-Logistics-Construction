Featured Project: Juba Commercial Tower

A landmark of modern engineering in South Sudan's capital

The Juba Commercial Tower stands as a testament to our capability to deliver complex high‑rise structures in challenging environments. This 12‑story reinforced concrete building houses offices, retail spaces, and a rooftop conference center.

Key project highlights:

  • 12 stories, 45 meters height – the tallest commercial building in Juba
  • Advanced foundation engineering on compressible soils
  • Full MEP installations including backup power and HVAC
  • Curtain wall facade with energy‑efficient glazing
  • Completed 18 months ahead of schedule

This project exemplifies our ability to combine international standards with local expertise, delivering a structure that has become a new landmark for the city.
