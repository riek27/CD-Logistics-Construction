About CD Logistics

Building South Sudan’s future with integrity, excellence, and reliability

Our History

A legacy of trust and quality since 2012

Founded in Juba, CD Logistics, Construction & General Trading Co. Ltd began as a small general trading outfit. Through unwavering commitment to quality and safety, we rapidly grew into one of South Sudan’s most respected construction and logistics firms. We have successfully delivered hospitals, educational institutions, commercial high‑rises, and critical infrastructure — always on time and within budget.

Our journey from a modest startup to a market leader is a testament to our dedication to excellence. We have built long‑term relationships with clients, suppliers, and communities by consistently exceeding expectations. Today, we are proud to be the partner of choice for government agencies, international NGOs, and private developers across the region.
