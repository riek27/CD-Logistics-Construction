About CD Logistics

Building South Sudan’s future with integrity, excellence, and reliability

Our Story

CD Logistic construction and General Trading Co. Ltd was initiated in Juba in 2012. As one of South Sudan’s indigenous companies, we were officially registered by the Ministry of Legal Affairs and Constitutional Development in 2015. Our office is located in Munuki Suk Mel-ishya, 107 Road, next to Charter Bank.

We operate in three main business sectors: Logistics, Design, and Construction. Through unwavering commitment to quality and safety, we rapidly grew into one of the most respected construction and logistics firms in the region. We have successfully delivered hospitals, educational institutions, commercial high‑rises, and critical infrastructure — always on time and within budget.

Our journey from a modest startup to a market leader is a testament to our dedication to excellence. We have built long‑term relationships with clients, suppliers, and communities by consistently exceeding expectations. Today, we are proud to be the partner of choice for government agencies, international NGOs, and private developers across South Sudan.

Our Approach

At CD Logistics, we believe that successful projects are built on a foundation of collaboration, precision, and transparency. Our comprehensive approach ensures a seamless experience from concept to completion.

Integrated Solutions: We combine construction expertise with logistics capabilities to offer end‑to‑end service, eliminating delays and reducing costs.

Meticulous Planning: Every detail is carefully considered — from material procurement to workforce management — ensuring efficient execution and risk mitigation.

Quality Assurance: Our team adheres to strict quality control protocols, using only premium materials and certified processes.

Client Partnership: We maintain open communication throughout the project, keeping you informed and involved at every stage.
